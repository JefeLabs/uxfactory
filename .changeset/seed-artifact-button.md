Seed button — one-click derive-from-project artifact drafts. Every missing
artifact row now shows a "Seed" action beside "Create": Seed dispatches a
generate-artifact job immediately (no interview) with guidance that instructs
the worker to derive the draft from the project's other registered artifacts
— classification, profile, brief, audience, personas, stories, features,
sitemap, brand, style — inferring sensible on-project defaults and marking
assumptions. Unlike Create, Seed never chains prerequisites; it plays off
whatever already exists. Create (the guided interview) stays available for
when you want to steer the details.
