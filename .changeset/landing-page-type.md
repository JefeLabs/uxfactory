New unit type: `landing-page` — the conversion-page hybrid (pages group,
channel DNA). A standalone campaign destination with one primary CTA, living
outside the IA tree: copy-deck and the design system are required and
load-bearing, stories are recommended (one conversion story is verifiable
but never forced), creative-brief is required-when-it-ships, and
sitemap/flows never appear. Composer droplist, CLI unit vocabulary, and
worker scope guidance all carry it.
